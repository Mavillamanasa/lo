DHANUSH

Dhanush is the youngest Indian actor to win the National Award for Best Hero. He won the award at the 58th National Film Awards for his role in Aadukalam, the Tamil-language drama directed by Vetrimaran.

AWARDS

1.14 SIIMA Awards
2.nine Vijay Awards
3.eight Filmfare Awards South
4.five Vikatan Awards

Dhanush has showcased his acting prowess in a variety of roles across different genres, from intense dramas to light-hearted comedies. His ability to portray diverse characters with depth and authenticity is remarkable
